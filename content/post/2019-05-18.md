## 英文短句：
> Time erodes all such beauty but what it cannot diminish is the wonderful workings of your mind, your humor, your kindness and your moral courage.

<!--more-->

> 时间可以吞噬一切，但它丝毫无法减少的是你伟大的思想，你的幽默，你的善良还有你的勇气。
