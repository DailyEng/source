## 英文短句：
> Do not look back and grieve over the past, for it’s gone. Live in the present, and make it so beautiful that it will be worth remembering.

<!--more-->

## 中文翻译：
> 耽溺回忆不如活在当下，让此刻成为未来荣耀之日。
