## 英文短句：
> Do not know how high the sky is until one climbs up the tops of mountains, and do not know how thick the earth is until one comes to the deep river.

<!--more-->

## 中文翻译：
> 不登高山，不知天之高也；不临深溪，不知天之厚也。
