## 英文短句：
> The foundation of knowledge must be laid by reading. General principles must come from books, which, however, must be brought to the test of real life.

<!--more-->

## 中文翻译：
> 读书是积累知识的基础。基础原理来源于书本，但须经实际生活的检验。
