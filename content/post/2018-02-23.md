## 英文短句：
> Friendship means understanding, not agreement. It means forgiveness, not forgetting. It means the memories last, even if contact is lost. 

<!--more-->

## 中文翻译：
> 友情是理解，不是妥协；是原谅，不是遗忘。即使不联系，感情依然在。
