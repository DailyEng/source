## 英文短句：
> If you want to build a ship, don't drum up people to collect wood and don't assign them tasks and work, but rather long for the endless immensity of sea.

<!--more-->

## 中文翻译：
> 如果你想造一艘船，先不要雇人收集木头，也不要给人分配任务，而是激发他们对海洋的渴望。
