## 英文短句：
> You must strive to find your own voice. Because the longer you wait to begin, the less likely you are to find it at all.

<!--more-->

## 中文翻译：
>  你们必须努力寻找自己的声音，因为你越迟开始寻找,找到的可能性就越小。
