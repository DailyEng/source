## 英文短句：
> No one is born hating another person because of the color of his skin. They can be taught to love, for love comes more naturally to the human heart.

<!--more-->

## 中文翻译：
> 没有人生来就因为肤色去憎恨别人。每个人都可以学会去爱，因为爱自在人心。
