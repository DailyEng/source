## 英文短句：
> There is nothing difficult for us to do in the world. If we do it, the difficult thing will be easy; if we do not, the easy thing will be difficult.

<!--more-->

## 中文翻译：
> 天下事有难易乎？为之，则难者亦易矣；不为，则易者亦难矣。
