## 英文短句：
> Most people grow up from sufferings, with optimistic attitude to struggle, one can thrive, otherwise one will be abandoned by his or her own life.

> 人是从苦难中成长起来的，唯有乐观奋斗，才能不断茁壮，反之则易埋没。
