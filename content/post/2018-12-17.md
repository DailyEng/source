## 英文短句：
> Swelled heads are so preoccupied with the few things they know, so that there is no room left for the innumerable things they don't know.

<!--more-->

## 中文翻译：
> 自命不凡者，脑中被其所知的少数事物所占据，以致没有空间去容纳无数其所不知的事物。
