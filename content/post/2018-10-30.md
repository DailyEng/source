## 英文短句：
> The defects and faults of the mind are like wounds in the body. After all imaginable care has been taken to heal them up, still there will be a scar left behind.

<!--more-->

## 中文翻译：
> 思想上的缺陷和弱点正如身体上的创伤，就算用尽一切办法将其治愈，仍然会留下疤痕。
