## 英文短句：
> Few things are impossible in themselves, and it is often for want of will, rather than of means, that man fails to succeed.

<!--more-->

## 中文翻译：
> 事情很少是不可能的；人们之所以不成功，与其说是条件不够，不如说是决心不够。
